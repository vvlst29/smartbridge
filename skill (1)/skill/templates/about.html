Project Overview

OptiCrop supports smarter, more sustainable farming decisions.

The system uses data-driven insights to help farmers, researchers, policymakers, and agricultural stakeholders understand crop-environment relationships.

Problem Addressed

Farmers often face losses because crop selection does not match soil nutrients, rainfall, temperature, humidity, or pH. OptiCrop converts these conditions into practical recommendations for better yield potential and resource efficiency.

Business Value

The platform reduces crop failure risk, supports fertilizer and water planning, and gives agricultural organizations a structured way to analyze production patterns for policy and planning decisions.

Technology Stack
